      About Converter

      How to use

      1. Drop or click to select files (images, audio, video, documents, or 3D models)
      2. Choose a target format from the dropdown
      3. Pick an output folder
      4. Click Convert

      Supported formats

      Image AVIF, BMP, GIF, HEIC, JPEG, JP2, JPEG XL, PDF, PNG, RAW (CR2/NEF/ARW/DNG), TIFF, WEBP, SVG
      Audio AAC, AIFF, FLAC, M4A, MP3, OPUS, Vorbis (OGG), WAV, WMA
      Video AVI, FLV, MKV, MOV, MP4, MPEG, OGV, WEBM, WMV, 3GP, GIF (video to GIF)
      Document PDF, TXT, DOCX, DOC
      3D Model 3DS, 3MF, AC, AMF, ASE, B3D, BLEND, BVH, COB, Collada (DAE), DXF, FBX, GLTF, GLB, LWO, MD2, MD5Mesh, MDC, MDL, MS3D, NFF, OBJ, OFF, Ogex, PLY, Q3O, SIB, SMD, STL, X, XGL
      Game Engine FBX, OBJ, MD5, SMD, B3D, MDL, MD2, MD3, 3DS, X, DXF + many more via assimp
      Animation Lottie (.lottie), SVG, APNG, Animated WebP

      Tips

      • Hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple files
      • 3D models can be converted to GLTF, GLB, or STL
      • Lottie animations can be converted to MP4, GIF, or WEBP
      • Documents (PDF, TXT, DOCX) can be converted to PDF or TXT
      • Large video files may take longer to convert
      • Output files are saved in your chosen folder
